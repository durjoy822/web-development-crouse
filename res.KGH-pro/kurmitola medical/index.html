Welcome to the official website of 500 Beded Kurmitola General Hospital.

History of KGH:

Kurmitola General Hospital’s construction work had started in the year 2004 and completed 2011. It was inaugurated by H.E. Sheikh Hasina, The Honorable Prime Minister of Bangladesh on 13 May 2012. This hospital started out door service in 12 November, 2012 and indoor service in 16 June 2013. The objectives of this hospital, to provide medical treatment for all citizens of the country, to provide emergency and life saving treatment to the road traffic accident victims of the nearby area, provide “on job training’’ to the internees and practical training to the cadets of AFMC and AFMI. It acts as the teaching hospital for Armed forces medical college.
